Upper Pennisula, MI

I grew up in Michigan, I may be a little bias towards it but it has some spetacular views. I remember spending summers up north in the U.P. and loved every moment of it. The fishing is is amazing from Rockbass to Northern Pike you will find it. there are numerous spots for hiking or for sightseeing. I can almost guarantee their is something for you do if you are a outdoorsy person.

West Virginia

There really isnt a single area of West Virgina that isnt beautiful. Tucked up in the Appalachian Mountains. The wild life and lack of people make this a great place to explore and go sightseeing from exploring caves to climbing mountains. As I said about the Upper Pennisula, you will find alot to do here also.
